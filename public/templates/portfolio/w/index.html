About the Artist

Hi, I'm Elena. I paint the world as it feels, not just as it looks.

My journey with watercolor began in a small sketchbook during a rainy summer. Since then, I've been obsessed with the unpredictable nature of water and pigment. I specialize in botanical illustrations, dreamlike landscapes, and capturing the quiet emotion in everyday scenes.
