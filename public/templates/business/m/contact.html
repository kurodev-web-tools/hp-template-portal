What is your typical project timeline?

Residential projects typically take 12-18 months from initial concept to completion. Commercial and cultural projects vary based on scale and complexity.

Do you work internationally?

Yes. We maintain studios in London, Tokyo, Berlin, New York, Oslo, Copenhagen, and Melbourne. Our team is equipped to handle projects globally.

What is your fee structure?

We offer fixed fee, percentage-based, and hourly structures depending on project scope. Contact us for a detailed proposal tailored to your needs.
