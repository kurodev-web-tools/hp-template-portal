The Studio

How We Work

Phase 01

Discovery

Every project begins with deep listening. We study the site, the context, and most importantly, the unspoken needs of those who will inhabit the space.

Phase 02

Distillation

Through iterative sketching and model-making, we reduce the program to its essential elements. What remains is architecture that cannot be otherwise.

Phase 03

Realization

Construction administration ensures every detail is executed with precision. We work closely with builders to maintain the integrity of the design vision.
