From Small Garden to Global Movement

Verdant Global started in a small community garden in Portland, where our founders grew organic herbs and vegetables using regenerative farming practices. What began as a local initiative to provide fresh, chemical-free produce to neighbors soon blossomed into something much larger.

Today, we partner with over 200 organic farms across 15 countries, bringing sustainably sourced products to conscious consumers while supporting fair trade practices and biodiversity conservation.
