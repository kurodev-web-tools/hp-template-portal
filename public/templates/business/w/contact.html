FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for academic access? expand_more
Academic access is available to doctoral candidates, postdoctoral researchers, and faculty members. Please submit your application through our online form, including your institutional affiliation, research proposal, and two letters of recommendation. Processing typically takes 4-6 weeks.
Are there fees associated with archive access? expand_more
Access fees vary by affiliation. Academic researchers from partner institutions receive complimentary access. Independent scholars may apply for day passes (£50) or annual memberships (£500). Digitization services and specialized assistance may incur additional charges.
Can I visit the physical archives in person? expand_more
Yes, qualified researchers may visit our reading rooms by appointment. Please note that some materials require 48-hour advance notice for retrieval. Special collections and fragile documents may have restricted handling requirements.
How can my institution become a partner? expand_more
Institutional partnerships are established through formal agreements. Contact our partnerships team to discuss collaboration opportunities, including faculty exchanges, joint research initiatives, and shared digitization projects.
