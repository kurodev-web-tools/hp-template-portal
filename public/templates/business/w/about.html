Our Story

Over a Century of Intellectual Discovery

Founded in 1892 by visionary scholars, Knowledge Plaza emerged from an unwavering belief in the transformative power of rigorous academic inquiry. What began as a modest collection of manuscripts has evolved into a world-renowned institution.

Our halls have welcomed Nobel laureates, pioneering researchers, and curious minds from every corner of the globe. Together, they have contributed to a living tradition of scholarship that continues to push the boundaries of human understanding.
