Our Story

Nexus AI was founded in 2019 by a team of former Google and OpenAI researchers who shared a common frustration: the most powerful AI tools were locked behind complex APIs and required PhD-level expertise to operate.

We set out to build a platform that brings the power of neural networks and large language models to everyday business users. No code required. No data science team needed. Just plug in your data and get insights.

Today, we serve over 10,000 enterprises across 50 countries, processing billions of data points daily to help companies make smarter, faster decisions.
