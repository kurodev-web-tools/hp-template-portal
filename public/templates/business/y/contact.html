FAQ

How do we start a project? expand_more

Begin by submitting an inquiry through this form. Our team reviews each project carefully to ensure alignment with our design philosophy. We typically respond within 5-7 business days to schedule an initial consultation.

What is your fee structure? expand_more

Fees are calculated as a percentage of construction costs, varying by project type and scope. Residential projects typically range from 12-18%, while cultural institutions are quoted individually. We provide detailed proposals after initial consultation.

Do you work internationally? expand_more

Yes. With seven studios globally, we regularly execute projects across continents. We partner with local architects of record to ensure compliance with regional building codes and regulations.

What is your design timeline? expand_more

Residential projects typically require 4-8 months for design development. Cultural and commercial projects vary widely based on scope. Construction administration is an additional service we provide throughout the build phase.
