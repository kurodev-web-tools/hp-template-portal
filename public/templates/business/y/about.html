Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

YIELD_GROWTH was founded in 2004 by James Richardson, a former Goldman Sachs portfolio manager with a vision: to make institutional-quality investment strategies accessible to individual investors.

Starting with just three clients and a commitment to excellence, Richardson built a firm grounded in transparency, disciplined risk management, and unwavering fiduciary responsibility.

Today, we manage over $2.5 billion in assets and serve more than 15,000 clients worldwide, while maintaining the personalized attention and care that defined us from day one.
