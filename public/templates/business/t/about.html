Our Story

From a single vision to a global legacy.

In 1924, master craftsman Henri Delacroix opened a modest workshop in the Marais district of Paris. His vision was simple yet radical: create objects of such perfect proportion and pristine quality that they would transcend trends and time.

A century later, that vision has blossomed into a global house of refinement. From private estates in the Swiss Alps to corporate sanctuaries in Tokyo, LUXE continues to define what it means to live with intention and elegance.

Each project we undertake carries the DNA of our heritage – an unwavering commitment to craftsmanship, an eye for essential beauty, and the wisdom to know that true luxury lies in what can be removed, not added.
