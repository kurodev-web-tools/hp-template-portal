FREQUENT QUERIES

How do I book artists for events? expand_more
Submit a booking inquiry through this form with your event details, budget range, and preferred dates. Our artist relations team responds within 48 hours with availability and rates.
Can I volunteer at events? expand_more
Yes! We have a volunteer program for dedicated community members. Volunteers receive free entry to events in exchange for assistance with setup, guest management, or breakdown.
How do I become a member? expand_more
Membership is currently invite-only with quarterly opening periods. Join our newsletter to be notified when applications open. Priority goes to active community participants and event attendees.
Do you accept demo submissions? expand_more
Yes, we accept demos every first Monday of the month. Send a private SoundCloud or Dropbox link with 3-5 tracks. We respond to all submissions within 2 weeks.
