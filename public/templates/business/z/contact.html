Inquiries

Common Questions

What is the typical investment for an executive retreat?

Our residential retreats begin at $35,000 for a 5-day immersion at our Kyoto sanctuary. Custom programs and extended stays are available upon consultation.

Do you offer virtual programs?

While we believe in the transformative power of physical presence at our sanctuaries, we do offer follow-up virtual coaching sessions for retreat alumni.

Is ZEN_GARDEN affiliated with any religion?

No. While our practice draws wisdom from Zen Buddhism and other contemplative traditions, we welcome leaders of all backgrounds. Our approach is secular and inclusive.

What can I expect during my first visit?

Begin with silence. Your initial consultation — whether virtual or in-person — starts with a guided meditation, followed by deep listening. We seek to understand your unique situation before proposing any path forward.
