Our Story

From a single brush to a global legacy.

In 1924, master lacquer artisan Takeshi Kousei opened a modest workshop in the historic Higashiyama district of Kyoto. His vision was simple yet profound: to create objects that embody the silence between breaths, the space between thoughts.

A century later, that vision has blossomed into a collective of master artisans spanning ceramics, textiles, metalwork, and lacquer. From tea ceremonies in Tokyo to private collections in Paris, Kousei continues to define what it means to live with intention and beauty.

Each piece we create carries the DNA of our heritage – an unwavering commitment to traditional techniques, an eye for essential beauty, and the wisdom to know that true craftsmanship lies not in what is added, but in what can be removed.
