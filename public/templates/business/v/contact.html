Inquiries

Common Questions

What is the typical timeline for a custom commission?

expand_more
Custom commissions typically require 3 to 6 months, depending on complexity and the artisan's schedule. Lacquerware pieces may require additional time for proper curing.

Do you ship internationally?

expand_more
Yes, we offer insured international shipping with white-glove delivery service. Our logistics team handles all customs documentation and ensures safe arrival.

Can I visit the Kyoto studio?

expand_more
Studio visits are by appointment only. We offer private viewings with our master artisans for serious collectors and commission inquiries.

How do I care for urushi lacquerware?

expand_more
Urushi pieces should be wiped clean with a soft, dry cloth. Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight and extreme temperatures. With proper care, lacquerware can last for generations.
