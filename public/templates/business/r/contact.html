Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services.

How quickly can you arrange international shipping?

expand_more
For air freight, we can typically arrange pickup within 24-48 hours of booking confirmation. Sea cargo bookings usually require 3-5 days advance notice. Our team will work with you to expedite urgent shipments whenever possible.

Do you provide customs clearance services?

expand_more
Yes, we offer comprehensive customs brokerage services in over 220 countries. Our licensed customs brokers handle documentation, duty calculations, and clearance procedures to ensure smooth cross-border shipping.

Can I track my shipment in real-time?

expand_more
Absolutely. All shipments include real-time tracking through our online portal and mobile app. You'll receive status updates at key milestones and can monitor your cargo's location 24/7.

What types of cargo do you handle?

expand_more
We handle a wide variety of cargo including standard freight, temperature-controlled goods, hazardous materials, oversized cargo, and high-value items. Our team can advise on special handling requirements for your specific cargo type.

How do I request a shipping quote?

expand_more
You can request a quote through our online form, by calling our customer service line, or by emailing your requirements. Please provide details about your cargo, origin, destination, and preferred timeline for the most accurate quote.
