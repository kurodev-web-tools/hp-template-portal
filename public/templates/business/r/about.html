Our Story

Building Bridges Across Continents

For over 30 years, we've been the trusted partner for businesses moving across borders.

Since 1994

From Humble Beginnings to Global Leader

Global Logistics started with a single truck and a vision to simplify cross-border commerce. What began as a family-owned regional carrier has grown into one of the world's most trusted logistics networks.

Today, we operate 1,500 hubs across 220 countries, delivering over 5 million packages annually. But our mission remains unchanged: to make global trade accessible, reliable, and efficient for businesses of all sizes.
